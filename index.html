Suspension Yoga Teacher Trainings

OmGym® Suspension Yoga™ is a multi-sensory immersion into enriched postural variation. This method includes a comprehensive mix of grounded and aerial asana that stretches, strengthens, balances, and restores the entire body, especially the core and stabilizing muscles.

Strategic use of the OmGym® Suspension System comfortably supports the body to encourage spinal decompression and a deeper exploration of each posture.
